Spirulae-Splat Viewer

Based on WebGL 3D Gaussian Splat Viewer by Kevin Kwok.

Use mouse or arrow keys to navigate.
movement (arrow keys) - left/right arrow keys to strafe side to side - up/down arrow keys to move up/down - space to jump camera angle (wasd) - a/d to turn camera left/right - w/s to tilt camera up/down - q/e to roll camera counterclockwise/clockwise - i/k and j/l to orbit trackpad - scroll to move back/forward - ctrl key + scroll to move forward/back at a higher rate - pinch to move forward/back - shift + scroll to move up/down or strafe mouse - click and drag to orbit - right click (or ctrl/cmd key) and drag up/down to move touch (mobile) - one finger to orbit - two finger pinch to move forward/back - two finger rotate to rotate camera clockwise/counterclockwise - two finger pan to move side-to-side and up-down gamepad - if you have a game controller connected it should work
